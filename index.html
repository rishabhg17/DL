RWA app

Irritated of going home to home and ringing doorbells trying to find who parked behind your car? Isn’t it annoying when someone blocks your way in the parking lot and now you can’t move your car?

We know what aggravates you and we came up with a solution to weed out your parking blues. RWA allows you to know exactly who blocked your way and gets you going within minutes. Here's how it works: The next time you see someone has parked his or her car behind yours, and you don’t know whose it is, simply open the RWA app on your phone, scan the number plate of the car and you will get the name and number of the owner.

Every person who registers with RWA has to provide their name, phone number and car number. On scanning the number plate of the car, the user will be able to see the name and number of the owner. The user can access these details and conveniently talk to them. Voila, a hassle of panic and confusion reduced to a series of two basic steps.

A one scan solution to the most common parking problem, RWA finishes the tedious task of finding the owner of the car in a few seconds and hence, no more knocking on doors or searching for the guard. So let us take your stress away and enjoy the ride ahead.
